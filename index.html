Member Spotlight

Valley Grind Expands to Chester

Congratulations to IVCC members Rachael & Travis Goings on the exciting expansion of their business! The owners of The Valley Grind in Greenville have opened a second location in Chester, now renamed The Lake Grind, formerly known as Coffee Station.

We love celebrating the continued growth of our IVCC members. Congratulations to Rachael and Travis—we're excited to see what's next.

Community Appreciation

Thank You, Lisa

IVCC sends warm wishes to Lisa Forcino, longtime owner of Crescent Country, as the shop moves into escrow and she begins her next chapter.

Thank you, Lisa, for your years of business, hospitality, and support of Indian Valley. We wish you every happiness in your next chapter.

Growing Our Community

Welcome New IVCC Members

Greenville Fire Cat Sanctuary

Please join us in welcoming the Greenville Fire Cat Sanctuary to the IVCC! Led by Kimberly Price and local volunteers, the sanctuary rescues and cares for cats displaced by the Dixie Fire while supporting trap-neuter-return efforts and finding homes for socialized kittens.

Crescent Mills Hotel & Store

We're excited to welcome the Crescent Mills Hotel & Store to the IVCC! The beautifully restored store is now open with coffee and fresh sandwiches, while work continues on the historic hotel, which will welcome guests soon.
